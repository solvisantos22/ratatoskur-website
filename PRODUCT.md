# Ratatoskur — Product context

Ratatoskur is an AI math coach for handwritten student work (Icelandic): students upload a problem, solve it by hand with Apple Pencil, and get mode-specific feedback (Hint / Check / Reveal). This repo is the **project-hub website** for it.

**Register:** brand (design IS the product — a marketing/hub site).

**Primary audience:** school administrators / decision-makers (the people we want to contact us), then teachers & students, then funders (Tækniþróunarsjóður Fræ → Sproti) and researchers.

**Primary action:** Contact us ("bring Ratatoskur to your school").

**Languages:** English default, Icelandic via toggle.

**Goals:** explain clearly in under a minute of scrolling; show the real product; make it easy for a school to reach out; reflect the existing brand faithfully.

**Non-goals (v1):** app distribution, accounts/auth, a CMS.
